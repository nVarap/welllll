Jobify Backend

The internship feature

  1. Jobify offers a unique Tinder-like feature for students to swipe left or right on internships, streamlining the internship search process.
  2. Through the swipe feature, students can quickly browse through internship opportunities and indicate their interest with a simple swipe.
  3. Internship listings on Jobify can view students' grades, providing them with insight into the academic performance of potential candidates.
  4. This feature facilitates a more personalized and efficient internship matching process, enhancing students' chances of securing relevant opportunities.

The assignments and grades feaature

  1. Jobify's grading and assignments features enable seamless submission and grading processes for students and teachers.

The schedule feature

  1. Jobify's schedule feature empowers students to manage their time effectively by creating personalized schedules.
  2. Students can input their class schedules and extracurricular activities into the platform to visualize their daily routines.
  3. The schedule feature includes countdown timers, allowing students to track how much time they have left in each class or activity.
  4. By providing a comprehensive overview of their commitments, Jobify helps students prioritize tasks and stay organized.
